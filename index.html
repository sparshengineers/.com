About the Practice

A Pune practice,
personally built.

Sparsh Engineers and Consultants is a Pune-based structural engineering and construction consultancy proprietored by Upesh Shah. We work directly with homeowners, builders and developers — translating architectural drawings into structurally sound, buildable plans, and staying on site through execution to handover.

No layers, no hand-offs. Every project that carries the Sparsh name is reviewed personally by Upesh Shah, from the first structural calculation to the final quality check on site.

  • Proprietor-ReviewedEvery design and site decision passes through Upesh Shah directly.
  • Transparent EstimatesCosts itemised by quantity, so there are no surprises mid-project.
  • Code-Compliant DesignStructural drawings prepared to relevant building-code standards.

What We Do

Services, by the sheet.

Each engagement is scoped and delivered like a drawing set — defined, numbered, and signed off.

SEC‑01

Structural Design & Detailing

RCC structural design, load calculations and detailed working drawings for residential and commercial buildings.

SEC‑02

Construction Project Management

End-to-end coordination of contractors, schedules and site activity, so the build stays on plan.

SEC‑03

Site Supervision & Quality Control

Regular on-site inspection of materials and workmanship, checked against drawing and code requirements.

SEC‑04

Estimation & Costing

Detailed quantity take-offs and cost estimates, so budgets are set before the first brick is laid.

SEC‑05

Government Liaison & Approvals

Documentation and follow-up for building-plan approvals and statutory NOCs with local authorities.

SEC‑06

Renovation & Structural Repairs

Structural assessment, retrofitting and remodelling for ageing or damaged buildings.
